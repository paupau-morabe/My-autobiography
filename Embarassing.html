Embarassing Moment

My most embarrassing experience is still fresh in my mind. I've spent my entire life trying to put this moment behind me, but it just won't go away. When I recall that day, I can't help but laugh. I experienced this awkward situation in elementary school. Because it was a Scouting Day, everyone was urged to take part in the activities. Yes, I did sign up, but the moment the race started, I stumbled and fell to the ground. I immediately started crying because everyone was gazing at me.
